10. Controls for do-not-track features

Most web browsers include a Do-Not-Track (“DNT”) feature you can activate to signal your preference not to have your online activity monitored. No uniform technology standard for recognizing DNT signals has been finalized, so we do not currently respond to them. California law requires that we disclose this. If a legal standard for DNT is adopted, we will update this notice accordingly.
