About

Three things most career strategists can't offer simultaneously

I'm Aaron Graham, a career strategist and AI consultant based in Maryland. Before founding Navigato, I spent years as a plaintiff's attorney and director of legal recruiting, then built a career development practice advising hundreds of professionals annually at one of the country's leading law schools.

That background gives me something rare: a lawyer's discipline for building arguments, an insider's knowledge of what actually gets people hired, and a practitioner's daily fluency with the AI tools reshaping how we work and search.

At Navigato I work with a deliberately small number of clients at a time. That's not a limitation, it's the model. Your situation deserves a strategy built around you, not a template dressed up to look like one.
